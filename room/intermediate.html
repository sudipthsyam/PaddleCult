Intermediate Surf Lesson

Description:

Welcome to the intermediate surf lesson! Enhance your paddle strength and practice turtle rolls for navigating through waves. Refine your pop-up technique for quicker and smoother transitions to standing. Work on turning and trimming on the face of the wave to control your speed and direction. Explore different board types and learn to read the lineup for more challenging waves. Continuously build confidence and enjoy progressing to the next level of surfing!

We highly recommend enrolling in a 2-week intermediate surf course to systematically refine your skills. Over the first week, you'll focus on perfecting essential techniques, including paddling, turtle rolls, and advanced pop-ups. The second week delves into more advanced maneuvers, exploring various board types, and fine-tuning your style in diverse wave conditions. This extended timeframe ensures a thorough progression, providing a strong skill set for intermediate surfers.

Explore the comprehensive details of our 2-week surf course here!

Week 1: Skill Refinement

Day 1-3:Focus on refining paddling techniques and practicing turtle rolls for wave navigation.

Day 4: Introduce advanced pop-up variations and work on quicker transitions

Day 5: Begin learning basic turning maneuvers and trimming on small waves.

Week 2: Advanced Techniques

Days 6-8: Dive into more challenging waves to master bottom turns, cutbacks, and riding on the wave face.

Day 9 Explore different board types and understand how they affect performance.

Days 10-11: Enhance style and flow, incorporating aerial tricks and maneuvers.

Days 12-14: Practice in varied conditions, fine-tuning skills and building confidence in handling different surf scenarios

By the end of this two-week intermediate surf lesson, you'll have developed a comprehensive set of skills to navigate and ride waves confidently, laying a solid foundation for your continued surfing journey.
