Paddlecult Surf N Stays

Set in Varkala, 500 metres from Odayam Beach, Paddle Cult (Surf N Stay) offers accommodation with a garden, free private parking, a terrace and a restaurant. The property is around 47 km from Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple, 47 km from Napier Museum and 1.9 km from Janardhanaswamy Temple. The accommodation provides room service and free WiFi throughout the property. Popular points of interest near the resort include Varkala Beach, Aaliyirakkm Beach and Varkala Cliff.
