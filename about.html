About

Welcome to PaddleCult

Welcome to Paddlecult, a surf school located in the charming coastal town of Varkala, known for its breathtaking views and stunning beaches. Our primary focus is to offer fun, safe, and professional surf lessons for all ages and abilities. We understand that surfing is not just a sport but a way of life, and that's why we are dedicated to sharing our passion for the ocean with everyone who comes through our doors. Our team is led by Blacky , our head surf coach with over 10 years of experience in surf coaching. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced surfer, our team will help you to get the most out of your surfing experience. So come and join us at Paddlecult - this is surfing, and we are stoked!.
